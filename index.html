    Ultimate Frisbee

    One of my other hobbies is Frisbee Ultimate. This sport is similar to American football in terms of pitch layout and rules. Instead, Frisbee Ultimate is a non-contact sport. The aim is to get the Frisbee in the opponents dead-zone to score a point, when the Frisbee is in your possession you are not allowed to move and must pass it to your teammates before the opponent counts to 10, this is called a stall out. I currently play Frisbee with the DCU Ultimate Society, it is very fun and I meet new people constantly.
